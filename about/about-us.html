Who We Are

Seven Hills Food Solutions is a premier agricultural export company dedicated to delivering the finest quality produce to markets worldwide. Our commitment to excellence spans from seed selection to final delivery.

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner for international clients seeking premium agricultural products that meet the highest standards of quality and food safety.

Seven Hills is promoted and supported by the VIPANI GLOBAL Group of Companies and has seen an average growth of more than 22% per year.

The VIPANI GLOBAL Group is driven by ethics and vision and is specialized in managing diversified food products with international companies in manufacturing and supply chain management.

Our Core Values

Principles that guide everything we do

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every product undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets international standards and exceeds customer expectations.

Ethical Business

Integrity and transparency are at the heart of our operations. We build lasting relationships through honest, ethical business practices.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and ensure long-term viability of agricultural resources.

Innovation

We embrace modern technology and innovative methods to enhance productivity, quality, and efficiency across our operations.

Customer Focus

Our customers' success is our success. We work closely with clients to understand and meet their specific needs and requirements.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our business, from cultivation and processing to packaging and delivery.
