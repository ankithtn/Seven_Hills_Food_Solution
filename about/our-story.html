Our Story

From a small beginning rooted in craftsmanship to a global export-focused food solutions company.

Early Days

A Simple Beginning

It began with an individual experiment — crafting a pickle so distinct in flavor and quality that it quickly gained attention within local communities abroad.

Growth

Demand Beyond Borders

What started as a small operation soon crossed local boundaries. Shipments expanded, partnerships formed, and products began reaching international markets from Tokyo to Texas.

Foundation

The Birth of Seven Hills

To meet growing global demand with consistency and structure, Seven Hills Food Solutions was established — focused on preserved vegetables and niche food products for international partners.

Today

Built for Global Supply

Today, Seven Hills operates with an end-to-end approach — from cultivation and processing to export-ready supply — serving retail, distribution, and food service partners worldwide.
