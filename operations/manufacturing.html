Manufacturing

Seven Hills Food Solutions is one of India’s leading exporters of premium pickles, gherkins, and preserved vegetables. With over 5.5 years of experience, we manage the complete journey — from seed to shelf — across our own farms and four state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in South India.

We supply private-label and bulk products to supermarkets, food-service chains, and industrial customers in over 40 countries, including the USA, UK, Germany, Australia, Canada, and the Middle East.

Integrated Processing

Controlled processing environments designed to preserve taste, texture, and quality at scale.

Export-Ready Facilities

Facilities built to meet international food safety, hygiene, and export compliance standards.

Scalable Capacity

Capable of handling bulk volumes while maintaining consistency across batches and markets.

Our Manufacturing Footprint

Our manufacturing operations are strategically located in South India, close to cultivation regions, ensuring freshness, efficient logistics, and tight quality control throughout the production cycle.

  • 4 manufacturing facilities in South India
  • Proximity to owned and partner farms
  • Designed for private-label and bulk production
