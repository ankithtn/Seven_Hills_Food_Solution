Seed to Shelf

Our Journey from Farm to Your Table

Complete Control, Exceptional Quality

From the moment a seed is planted to when our products reach your shelf, every step is carefully monitored and perfected. Our vertically integrated process ensures quality, freshness, and food safety at every stage.

01

Cultivation

Our journey begins in carefully selected farms across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. Seeds are planted using cluster sowing methods, ensuring optimal growth conditions.

  • Contract farming across 225 acres
  • Compliance with international standards
  • Sustainable farming practices
02

Harvest

Vegetables are hand-picked at peak physiological maturity by trained workers. Each product undergoes immediate visual inspection for size, firmness, and quality.

  • Hand-picked at optimal maturity
  • Immediate quality inspection
  • Zero-tolerance rejection policy
03

Transportation

Fresh produce is transported quickly to processing facilities. Our efficient logistics ensure minimal time between harvest and processing to preserve freshness.

  • Short lead time from farm to facility
  • Temperature-controlled vehicles
  • Real-time tracking and monitoring
04

Processing

Fresh greens are washed, sorted, brined, fermented, and pasteurized. Our state-of-the-art HACCP-certified facilities ensure every step is controlled.

  • Multi-stage washing and sorting
  • Controlled fermentation process
  • Pasteurization for food safety
05

Quality Control

Quality checks ensure pH levels, microbial safety, and flavor consistency. Our laboratory tests every batch for compliance with international standards.

  • pH level testing for preservation
  • Microbiological safety verification
  • Flavor and texture consistency checks

Ready for Your Shelf

From seed to shelf, every step is designed to deliver exceptional quality.
