Quality & Food Safety

Our unwavering commitment to excellence at every stage

Excellence is Not Negotiable

At Seven Hills Food Solutions, quality and food safety aren't just policies, they're the foundation of everything we do. From the moment a vegetable is harvested to its arrival at your facility, every step is monitored, tested, and perfected.

Our zero-compromise approach has earned us the trust of leading international retailers and the highest certifications from world-renowned auditing bodies.

Our Standards

Strict Selection Criteria

Hand-Picked Excellence

Only the freshest, hand-picked vegetables at physiological maturity enter our facility. We reject anything that doesn't meet our strict visual, size, and firmness criteria.

Visual Inspection

Every vegetable is visually examined for color, texture, and absence of defects. No blemishes, discoloration, or damage is acceptable.

Size & Grade Standards

Precise sizing and grading ensure consistency. Each product category has specific dimensional requirements that must be met.

Firmness & Maturity

Products are tested for optimal firmness and physiological maturity. Only vegetables at peak condition are processed.

Zero Tolerance Rejection

Any product that fails even one criterion is immediately rejected. No exceptions, no compromises—ever.

Our Process

Quality at Every Stage

From farm to final packaging—monitored, tested, perfected

01

Farm Selection

Only approved farms with documented practices. Soil testing, water quality checks, and farming method verification.

02

Harvest Inspection

Visual inspection, maturity assessment, and immediate quality grading at harvest point. Rejected products never enter the facility.

03

Facility Intake

Secondary verification at facility gates. Temperature checks, contamination screening, and traceability documentation.

04

Processing

HACCP-controlled processing lines. Continuous monitoring of temperature, hygiene, and process parameters. Regular testing.

05

Quality Testing

Laboratory testing for microbiological safety, chemical residues, and quality parameters. No batch ships without clearance.

06

Final Packaging

Export-grade packaging in controlled environments. Final inspection, labeling verification, and cold chain monitoring.
